Bio

Founding member of both Tiny Danza and Dufferin '83, Andrew Cameron makes his solo debut with the self-directed video "Want It All" and Ep "Coyote Off A Cliff". Some past collaborations include Jimmy Harry (Pink, Ke$ha, Madonna), two time Juno nominee Fresh Kils and Juno nominated Alyssa Reid. Coyote Off A Cliff features production from Australia's M-PHAZES and Cam Bluff, Toronto's own Rich Kidd and Myer Clarity, and Think Twice.
