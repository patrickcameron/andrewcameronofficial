Bio

Founding member of both Tiny Danza and Dufferin '83, Andrew Cameron makes his solo debut with the self-directed video "Want It All", and EP, "Coyote Off A Cliff." Some past collaborations include Jimmy Harry (Pink, Ke$ha, Madonna), two time Juno nominee Fresh Kils and Juno nominated Rich Kidd. "Coyote Off A Cliff" features production from Austrailia's MPHAZE and Cam Bluff, Toronto's own Rich Kidd, Marlon James and Think Twice. “Dark Matters” Cameron’s second project, is expected fall 2016. (more...)

"Acting, writing, singing, directing, anything it’s the same. I just want to create and put it out. Business is super creative. Planning heists is creative... For music, I was always influenced more by songs then by a whole albums or artists. Atlantic City - Bruce Springsteen, Papa's Got A Brand New Bag - James Brown, Steamroller - James Taylor, Debra - Beck. Every one of those artists is just as responsible as me for anything I write. Dj Shadow said it best on his record: “I would like to be able to continue to let what is inside of me, which comes from all the music that I hear, I would like for that to come out. It's like, it's not really me that's coming through.. The music's coming through me...." I try to just go with what feels good for me but pop restricts you. You gotta account for that and make decisions that honor that formula. I’ve written a ton of weird, useless songs as a reaction to always trying to write radio pop. One day they will all drop in one album... Sometime soon...”
